What is NFTNDR

NFTinder is an idea inspired by a new exploding crypto industry of non-fungible tokens. So many ordinary investors are trying to get into NFTs, but right now it can be extremely overwhelming all the different platforms, thousands of different NFT Collections. It's hard to understand where to start NFTNDR is an application which is dedicated to ease the entry level for new investors and thus immensely expand the market capitalization.
